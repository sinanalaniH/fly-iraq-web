Our Story

Born in Baghdad,
flying for Iraq

Fly Iraq First was founded with a single belief: that Iraq deserves an airline as proud and welcoming as its people. From the ancient banks of the Tigris to the great cities of the world, we carry that spirit on every flight.

We blend modern, international standards of safety and comfort with hospitality that could only come from home — making every journey unmistakably Iraqi.
