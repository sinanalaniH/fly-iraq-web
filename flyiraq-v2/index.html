Our story

Carrying the pride of Iraq, higher

Fly Iraq First started with a simple belief: that Iraqis deserve an airline that truly feels like their own. We're proud to call Baghdad home, and we try to carry a little of that warmth onto every single flight — whether you're heading off to see family, flying out to close a deal, or finally taking the trip you've been promising yourself for years.

Our people are Iraqi, our welcome is Iraqi, and the care you feel on board isn't something we think you can fake. We're still a young airline, and we're growing carefully — adding routes, aircraft and people who believe in doing things properly. Thank you for flying with us. We honestly don't take it for granted.
