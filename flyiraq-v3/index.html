About Us

Carrying the pride of Iraq

Fly Iraq First is Iraq's premium airline, born in Baghdad and built to connect our homeland with the world. We combine modern aircraft, international safety standards, and the warmth of genuine Iraqi hospitality.

From the ancient cradle of civilisation, our mission is simple: to give every traveller a world-class journey they can be proud of — whether flying for business, family, or discovery.
